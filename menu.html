Chicken Marsala

Traditionally Chicken Marsala is an Italian-American dish of golden pan-fried chicken cutlets and mushrooms in a Marsala wine sauce. Our chefs here at Marlene have taken that ispiration and elevated it utlizing a Joyce Farms confit leg quarter, rich marsala sauce fortified with fresh aromatics and citrus. All paired with a wild mushroom and asparagus risotto.
